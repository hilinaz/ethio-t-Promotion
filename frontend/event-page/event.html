Historical Event

Ethiopian Christmas (Gena): Celebrated on January 7, Gena is a major religious and cultural event in Ethiopia. In Lalibela, known for its rock-hewn churches, the celebration is particularly vibrant, attracting pilgrims and tourists alike.

Cultural Event

Volcanic Activity and Evacuations in Afar Region: On January 3, 2025, signs of volcanic activity were observed near Mount Dofen in the Afar region, prompting the evacuation of approximately 80,000 residents as a precautionary measure. The government is closely monitoring the situation to mitigate potential risks.
